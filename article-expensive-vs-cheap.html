Expensive vs Cheap Kitchen Gadgets

The right question is not “cheap or expensive.” It is “where does spending more actually help?”

There are some categories where better materials and better heat control are worth paying for. There are others where a simple version does the job well enough.

Spend more on

  • Cookware that affects results
  • Air fryers and appliances with moving parts
  • Knives and core prep tools

Save money on

  • Basic utensils
  • Simple storage helpers
  • Non-precision accessories
