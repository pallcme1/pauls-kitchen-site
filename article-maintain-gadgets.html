How to Maintain Your Kitchen Gadgets

Maintenance is often the difference between a tool that lasts a year and one that lasts much longer.

  • Clean right after use when possible
  • Avoid harsh scrubbing on coated baskets and inserts
  • Store parts fully dry
  • Replace worn accessories before they become annoying

Good maintenance protects both performance and your willingness to keep using the tool.
