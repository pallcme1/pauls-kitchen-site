Traffic System

SEO + Pinterest + content loop

This is the practical visibility system for getting eyes on the site without turning it into spam.

SEO

Publish helpful clusters

For every buyer page, publish at least two supporting know-how pages and link them together.

Pinterest

Make 2 pins per post

One pin should emphasize the product angle, the other should emphasize the tip or outcome.

Social proof

Reuse evaluator voice

Your tone should sound like a practical reviewer, not a hype marketer.

Weekly publishing rhythm

  • Week 1: one buyer guide + one how-to article
  • Week 2: one comparison article + one maintenance or mistakes article
  • Week 3: refresh one older guide and add new internal links
  • Week 4: create pin graphics and share your strongest pages again

Traffic assets per article

  • 1 main article
  • 2 Pinterest pins
  • 1 short email blurb
  • 1 Facebook post or community share
  • 1 updated internal link from an older article
