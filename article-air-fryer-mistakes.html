Common Air Fryer Mistakes

A surprising number of air fryer complaints are really usage mistakes.

  • Overfilling the basket
  • Skipping a shake or turn when needed
  • Ignoring preheat on recipes that need it
  • Not cleaning enough between uses

When the food is crowded, airflow suffers. When the basket stays dirty, results get worse over time. A few small habits change the whole experience.
